About Us

Welcome to our Intrusion Detection & Prevention System (IDPS) web application! This platform is designed to provide real-time monitoring of incoming and outgoing IP traffic. Using packet sniffing and geolocation services, we detect suspicious activities, highlight potential threats, and display key IP metadata such as location and organization. The user-friendly dashboard includes interactive charts, tables, and alerts to empower security professionals in identifying and mitigating risks quickly and effectively.

Whether you're a network admin or cybersecurity enthusiast, this tool helps you stay vigilant, informed, and in control of your network's safety.
